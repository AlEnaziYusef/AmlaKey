## App Description (English)

Amlakey is the smart property management app designed for landlords and property owners in Saudi Arabia. Manage your properties, track tenants, collect rent, and monitor expenses — all from your phone.

**Key Features:**

- Property Management: Add and organize multiple properties with unit tracking, floor counts, and property types (apartments, villas, commercial buildings, shops)

- Tenant Tracking: Manage tenant information, lease dates, and payment history. Get alerts for lease expirations and overdue payments

- Rent Collection: Record payments, track collection rates, and view payment history per tenant or property

- Expense Management: Log maintenance, utilities, cleaning, and other expenses by property with category tracking

- Performance Dashboard: View collection rates, revenue, occupancy, and net income with customizable time periods

- Reports: Generate detailed property and financial reports

- Utility Integration: Track electricity (SEC) and water (NWC) accounts per property or per unit

- Data Backup: Export and restore your data securely

- Biometric Security: Lock the app with Face ID or fingerprint

- Bilingual Support: Full Arabic and English interface with RTL layout support

- Dark Mode: Comfortable viewing in any lighting

Whether you own a single apartment or manage a portfolio of properties, Amlakey helps you stay organized, collect rent on time, and make smarter decisions about your real estate investments.
